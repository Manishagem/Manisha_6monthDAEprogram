“Good morning, and thank you for joining us today.
My name is Manisha Gupta, and I have over eight years of experience in the IT industry as a software developer, working with technologies such as Robotic Process Automation using UiPath, ASP.NET, C#, and SQL.

I joined this six-month program with a clear goal — to transition into cybersecurity, specializing in Identity and Access Management, or IAM.

IAM is about ensuring the right person has the right access to the right resources at the right time for the right reason. IAM engineers help organizations secure their systems by managing digital identities and controlling access to applications and data.Their role is critical in enforcing security policies, preventing unauthorized access, and supporting compliance through technologies like SSO, MFA, and Role-Based Access Control.”

As part of this program, I focused on learning Python, AI tools, and cybersecurity concepts, and I built a capstone project called the Enterprise Identity Security Platform.

So before I explain the solution, let me first explain the problem.”

Slide 2 – Problem Statement (35 sec)

“In many organizations today, identity and access management is still handled across multiple systems, and access provisioning is often manual and time-consuming.

This leads to common issues such as delayed onboarding and offboarding, users having excessive access, and lack of centralized visibility into who has access to what. These issues create security risks and compliance challenges.

Organizations need a smarter and more automated way to manage user identities and access securely and efficiently.”

Slide 3 – Project – Enterprise Identity Security Platform (45 sec)

“To address this problem, I designed and built a prototype called the Enterprise Identity Security Platform.

This platform focuses on core IAM functionalities such as Role-Based Access Control, which ensures users get access based on their job role, Single Sign-On to improve user experience and security, Multi-Factor Authentication for stronger authentication, and passwordless authentication concepts.

The goal of this platform is to centralize identity management, automate access provisioning and deprovisioning, and improve overall organizational security.”

Slide 4 – Technical Overview (40 sec)

“From a technical perspective, I built this project using Python for backend logic and automation.

The system manages users, roles, and permissions, and demonstrates how access can be controlled and automated based on roles and policies.

I also explored how AI can be integrated into IAM. For example, AI can be used to analyze access requests, detect unusual access patterns, and help in making intelligent access decisions. This shows how AI and cybersecurity can work together to build smarter identity systems.”

Slide 5 – Business Value (35 sec)

“This project not only demonstrates technical implementation but also provides business value.

It helps organizations improve security and compliance, reduce manual effort in access management, enable faster onboarding and offboarding, and reduce the risk of unauthorized access.

This approach also supports the Zero Trust security model, where every access request is verified before access is granted.”

Slide 6 – Conclusion (25 sec)

“In conclusion, this cohort helped me combine software development, cybersecurity, automation, and AI to build a practical IAM solution.

My goal moving forward is to continue developing AI-driven IAM solutions and work in the field of Identity and Access Management and Cybersecurity, where I can contribute to building secure and scalable identity systems for organizations.

Thank you for your time, and I look forward to your questions.”

Very Important – How to Sound Confident

Remember these speaking tips:

Speak slowly
Pause after each slide
Emphasize these words:
Security
Automation
Identity
Access
Business Value
Do not rush the introduction
Smile when you start and when you end
One-Line Summary (If They Ask: “Explain your project in one line”)

Prepare this line:

“My project is an Identity and Access Management platform that automates user access control and improves organizational security.”

Memorize this line — it’s your power statement.
